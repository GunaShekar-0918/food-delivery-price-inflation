Task 2 – Exploratory Data Analysis Insights

1. The average food price inflation across delivery platforms is approximately ₹48, indicating that customers generally pay higher prices on food delivery apps compared to dine-in prices.

2. Swiggy shows a slightly higher average price inflation than Zomato, suggesting that pricing strategies vary across delivery platforms.

3. Cities like Pune and Bangalore have the highest price inflation, while cities such as Delhi and Mumbai show relatively lower inflation.

4. Barbecue and Indian cuisines experience the highest price inflation, whereas street food and fast food have comparatively lower inflation.

5. In all observed cases, the app price is higher than the dine-in price, confirming the presence of consistent price inflation on food delivery platforms.
