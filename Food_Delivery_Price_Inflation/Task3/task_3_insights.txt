Task 3 – Dashboard Insights

1. The KPI shows that customers pay an average of ₹47–₹50 extra per food item.
2. Swiggy shows slightly higher inflation compared to Zomato.
3. Barbecue and Indian cuisines show the highest inflation.
4. Certain cities like Pune and Bangalore show higher inflation.
5. Discounts displayed often do not reduce the final payable amount.

Conclusion:
Food delivery apps inflate prices compared to dine-in prices.

Recommendation:
Customers should compare dine-in and app prices before ordering food online.
